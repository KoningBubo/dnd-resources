#### Quill of Inspiration
*Wondrous Item, uncommon*
:
![item](https://github.com/KoningBubo/dnd-resources/blob/main/images/items/magical_items/uncommon/quill_of_inspiration.png?raw=true) {position:absolute,top:40px,left:480px,width:200px}
As an action, you can write a short phrase describing a creature's strength, talent or determination, such as "Strong as an ox", or "Quick as the wind".

Choose one ability score tied to the phrase.

:

For the next hour, one creature that reads the phrase can add 1d6 to one ability check or saving throw using the chosen score.

:

Once used, this property can't be used again until the next dawn.
