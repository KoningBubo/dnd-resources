#### Dagger of Misfortune
*Weapon, Rare (requires attunement)*
:
![item](https://github.com/KoningBubo/dnd-resources/blob/main/images/items/magical_items/rare/assassins_dagger.png?raw=true) {position:absolute,top:0px,left:400px,width:380px}
Those who feel the sting of this blade are quite unfortunate indeed.

:

***Beshaba's Fang.*** You can expend the magic contained in this mark to make your strike even more deadly.

When you hit with an attack roll, you can choose to expend this mark to add an additional 6d6 piercing damage on top of the regular damage.

Once this ability has been used, it can never be used again. If both marks have been used, the dagger crumbles to dust.

:

***Beshaba's Mask.*** You can expend the magic contained in this mark to change your appearance.

You cast Disguise Self and any Charisma checks you make to hide your true identity are made with advantage.

Once this ability has been used, it can never be used again. If both marks have been used, the dagger crumbles to dust.

:

***Accidental Death.*** Any creature you kill with this dagger looks as if it simply had an accident instead of being murdered.

A creature needs to pass a DC 17 Investigation check to learn that the victim was murdered.

:

***Lady Doom's Curse.*** Once per session, the DM can replace the result of a d20 roll of the wielder with a natural 1.
