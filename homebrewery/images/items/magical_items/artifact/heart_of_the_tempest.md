#### Heart of the Tempest
*Wondrous Item, Artifact (requires attunement)*
:
![item](https://github.com/KoningBubo/dnd-resources/blob/main/images/items/magical_items/artifact/heart_of_the_tempest.png?raw=true) {position:absolute,top:0px,left:420px,width:330px}
The heart of a storm primordial locked in a nigh-indestructible vial. The lightning bolts that flash inside do so in the rhythm of a heartbeat.

:

***Control Weather.*** You can cast the Control Weather spell at will. You can choose the temperature, but the precipitation and wind must increase.

:

***Elemental Spellcasting.*** The spellcasting ability when drawing power from the vial is Charisma (spell attack +11, spell save 19). You can cast the following spells, requiring no material components:

* At will: gust, control weather
* 1/day each: call lightning, sleet storm, storm of vengeance

:

***Emotional Attunement.*** This artifact reacts heavily to the emotions of nearby powerful air creatures (CR 10 or more).

Such creatures are instantly aware of the presence of this artifact and assume immediate control over it.
