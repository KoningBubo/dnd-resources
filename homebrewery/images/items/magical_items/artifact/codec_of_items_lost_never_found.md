#### Codec of Items Lost, Never Found
*Wondrous Item, Artifact*
:
![item](https://github.com/KoningBubo/dnd-resources/blob/main/images/items/magical_items/artifact/codec_of_items_lost_never_found.png?raw=true) {position:absolute,top:70px,left:420px,width:380px}
An ancient tome that guards the secrets of the most powerful magical items in existence. It does not share its secrets willingly...

:

When attempting to read this tome, one must first succeed on a DC 15 Sleight of Hand (thieves' tools) check to open the lock, followed by a DC 15 Intelligence, Wisdom or Charisma check to will the book into giving you information, followed by a DC 15 Investigation check to see through the illusory script that appears. All checks need to be passed in sequence by the same character.

After successfully learning a secret from the book, it slams shut and one must perform all checks again if one wants to read it again. The DC for all checks increases by 1. This DC resets back to 15 after a century, counting from the moment the DC increased for the first time.

:

***Forbidden Knowledge.*** After successfully passing all the checks, the book will reveal information about 1 random artifact that is suited to the reader.

:

***Reveal Thy Secrets.*** One can read this tome normally and freely if the lock is opened with the associated key.
